Philosophy

My name is Kesslere Delphonse and I am a cinematographer. I am currently from Miami, Florida. I was born to create dramatic and provocative images.

I believe in visuals…
I believe that in every single image there is a story to tell.

There is a world within that image that can transcend an individual to a place where they have never been before.

I believe that cinematography is the poetry of images because in every scene there is color to detract; there is camera movement to elevate the characters, and the use of lighting to visually push the scene to a new echelon in the audience’s eyes.

I strive to be a master of this craft.

I use my eyes to manipulate the unwanted interferences that block the direction of the moving image. I have experience in shooting in film and in HD digital. I’ve searched all my life to find my true purpose in this world; and by somehow by accident I fell upon cinematography-and it became the love of my life.
