Lou's Prey - Cameraman

Nominated for Best Cinematography AAU Epidemic Film Festival 2013, Official Selection of the Hollywood Black Film Festival. When Troy James decides to steal from his boss, notorious crime boss Big Reggie, he is a wanted man. Kuroda Red, Big Reggie's hired hit man, is hot on his trail. When he finally catches up to Troy the two men experience an accident and find out their fate in result of their wrong doings. Written and Directed by Juan Davis, @The1JuanDavis Director of Photography Keng Edited by Herb Sneed Produced by Stephan Gonzalez and Juan Davis Starring Xavier Galindo, Vint Carmona, Pete Johnson, Anneliese Heggli, Joe Calaviti, Marvin West, Thaddeus Dickens and Anju Hyppollite.
